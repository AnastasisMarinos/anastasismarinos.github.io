About Me

Hey! I’m Anastasis Marinos, a 20-year-old game developer from Greece. I’m passionate about building immersive, story-driven experiences. I mainly work in Unreal Engine, combining retro and stylized visuals—inspired by PSX-era aesthetics—with strong atmosphere and narrative design.


Alongside development, I use Blender for 3D work and FL Studio to create sound effects and music. I enjoy being involved in every layer of a project, shaping both the look and feel of the worlds I build.


I’m currently expanding my skills by studying filmmaking and storytelling, aiming to understand how cinematic techniques translate into interactive experiences. My goal is to eventually work as a game director, leading projects that blend visual style, sound, and narrative into cohesive and memorable games.


I’m passionate about games that make players feel something—whether through atmosphere, story, or style—and I’m always exploring new ways to bring that to life.


I’m always open to collaborations or freelance work.
