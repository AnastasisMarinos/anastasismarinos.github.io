Privacy Policy

Effective date: [2025-10-24]

Controller: Anastasis Marinos (sole developer)
Country: Greece (EU/EEA)
Website: anastasismarinos.com
Contact: contact@anastasismarinos.com

What this policy covers
This policy applies to my games (including the “Dead by Daylight Clone” prototype and future titles) and to anastasismarinos.com. Multiplayer is provided by Epic Online Services (EOS).

What I collect
I do not actively collect or store personal data. I don’t run analytics or ads, and I don’t set cookies beyond what’s strictly necessary to deliver the website.

Multiplayer via EOS
Online features (accounts, matchmaking, sessions) are powered by Epic Online Services. EOS may process limited information needed to make multiplayer work (e.g., Epic Account ID, display name, IP address/region for connectivity, session/lobby IDs). In some cases I may see limited identifiers or session metadata exposed by EOS only to operate multiplayer. I do not export, combine, sell, or use this for marketing. For details, see Epic’s Privacy Policy.

Website access
The site is minimal. Your IP address is necessarily processed by my hosting to deliver pages. I don’t retain this data or run analytics.

Legal basis (GDPR)
If personal data is processed to enable multiplayer, the legal basis is:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) – to provide online matchmaking/sessions you request.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) – to keep services secure and prevent abuse.

Sharing
Service providers: Epic Online Services (for multiplayer) and hosting/CDN (for the website). I may also share information if required by law or to protect rights and safety. I do not sell personal data.

International transfers
EOS and hosting providers may process data outside the EU/EEA using safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful mechanisms.

Retention
I do not keep player data. Any limited metadata visible through EOS is used only transiently to operate sessions. Website delivery logs are not retained by me.

Your rights (EU/EEA)
You can request access, deletion, rectification, restriction, or portability where applicable. Because I don’t keep personal data, I may ask you to use your Epic account tools or contact Epic directly for data they control. You can also contact me and I’ll help route your request. You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA).

Children
My games are not directed to children. I do not knowingly collect children’s data.

Security
I use reasonable measures to keep the services reliable and secure. No online service can be 100% secure.

Changes
If I change how I use data (for example, if I add my own accounts, analytics, or chat), I’ll update this page and change the effective date.

Contact
Questions about privacy? Email me at contact@anastasismarinos.com.
