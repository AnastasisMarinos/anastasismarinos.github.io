About Me

Personal Profile

I'm an Unreal Engine Developer/Designer from Greece with lots of experience in Blueprints and C++, designing and bringing to life various game concepts. Comfortable with game optimization, as well as documenting code and maintaining clean version control (Git/Perforce), to keep colleagues aligned. I’m an adaptable all-rounder with practical 3D and audio skills (Blender modeling, GIMP texturing, FL Studio/Audacity for sound). My goal is to blend cinematography in games without interrupting the gameplay loop with cutscenes.
