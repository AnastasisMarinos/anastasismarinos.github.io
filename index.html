About Me

Hey! I’m Anastasis Marinos, a 20-year-old game developer from Greece. I’m passionate about building immersive, emotional experiences through interactive worlds. My work focuses on gameplay systems, design, and storytelling — especially within horror and atmospheric genres.

I primarily work with Unity and Unreal Engine, exploring mechanics, shaders, and procedural systems. I also love experimenting with AI behaviors and environmental storytelling to create depth and tension in my games.

My dream is to continue developing games that challenge expectations and inspire other creators. I’m always open to collaborations or freelance work — feel free to reach out!
